Century Specialty Script is a Specialty Pharmacy that offers comprehensive pharmacological care for patients with acute and chronic illnesses. Through a comprehensive system of care, Century Specialty Script provides patients with expert therapy management services designed to meet their unique needs. Our experienced staff of Licensed Pharmacists, Pharmacy Technicians, Patient Care Coordinators, and Billing Specialists, provides superior clinical, economic and humanistic outcomes. The combined effect of our specialized services results in increased patient adherence and the maximization of overall therapy. Century Care AdherenceProgram Our Century Care Patient Adherence Program is individualized to meet the specific needs of our patients. Our programs feature medication reminders, medication specific literature, educational materials and updated information on local support organizations and events 24- Hour Clinical Assistance On-call pharmacists are available for counseling 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Dedicated Patient Care Coordinator A Patient Care Coordinator is assigned to each of our patients, enabling us to provide consistent individualized service and care. Insurance and Prior Authorization Our Patient Care Coordinators handle all aspects of insurance verification and necessary prior authorizations. In-Home Nursing Services When necessary, Home Health Nurses are available to visit our patients and instruct him/her on the proper techniques for injectable medications. Century Specialty Script Website Our website provides updated information on medication, disease states, patient education and links to national and local organizations that may be of interest to our patients. Single Pharmacy Solution Century Specialty Script can fill all of your prescription needs including, but not limited to, injectable and oral medications. * Same day delivery is available when necessary for start/continuation of therapy.
